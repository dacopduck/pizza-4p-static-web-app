History Of 4P'S

Peter Lutz grew up in Detroit and still has an affinity for the style of pizza his home city has popularized. He likes New York-style pizza and other varieties as well. Pizza comes in many styles, and Lutz isn’t one of those snobs who thinks his preferred style is the only kind of pizza worth eating. Having said that, there’s only one pizza that he has ever fallen in love with so deeply that it became an obsession-turned-profession.

Nowaday, Pizzeria Bruno carries both domestic and Italian wines, as well as a few from South America (Argentina and Chile) and New Zealand. But, for the most part, the offerings hail from Italy.
